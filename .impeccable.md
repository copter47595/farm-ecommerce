# Design Context: ฟาร์มพึ่งหนุ่ม

## Users & Purpose

**Target Audience:**
- Local Thai customers seeking fresh, organic farm products
- Health-conscious consumers who value quality and natural products
- People who want to support local farmers and sustainable agriculture
- Age range: 25-55, middle to upper-middle class

**Context of Use:**
- Browsing for fresh produce online (mobile-first, but also desktop)
- Looking for trustworthy, natural food sources
- Seeking convenience without sacrificing quality
- Want to feel connected to the source of their food

**Job to Be Done:**
- Find and purchase fresh, organic farm products easily
- Feel confident about product quality and farm practices
- Support local agriculture and sustainable farming

## Brand Personality

**Three Words:**
1. **Natural** - Genuine, unprocessed, close to nature
2. **Warm** - Friendly, approachable, human connection
3. **Trustworthy** - Reliable, honest, quality-focused

**Emotional Goals:**
- Make customers feel good about their purchase (supporting local farmers)
- Create a sense of freshness and health
- Build trust through transparency and authenticity
- Evoke the feeling of visiting a friendly neighborhood farm

**Tone of Voice:**
- Friendly and conversational (not corporate)
- Honest and transparent
- Knowledgeable but not preachy about organic/natural
- Warm Thai hospitality

## Aesthetic Direction

**Visual Tone:**
- **Natural & Organic:** Soft, earthy, inspired by farmland and nature
- **Warm & Inviting:** Like stepping into a cozy farm shop
- **Clean & Modern:** Professional but not sterile or corporate

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Olive green (#5D6E45, #6B7B53) - natural, muted, sophisticated
- **Secondary:** Warm cream/beige (#F5F1E8, #EDE8DB) - cozy, organic
- **Accent:** Terracotta/sienna (#C4705A) - earthy, warm
- **Text:** Deep brown (#3D3B30) - readable, warm, not harsh black
- **Background:** Warm off-white (#FAF8F3) - softer than pure white

**Typography Strategy:**
- **Headings:** Clean, slightly rounded sans-serif with personality (Sarabun or similar Thai-friendly)
- **Body:** Highly readable, good line-height (1.6-1.7), 16px minimum
- **Weight contrast:** Clear hierarchy with weight differences

**Anti-References (What to Avoid):**
- Bright/neon greens that feel artificial
- Corporate blue/gray color schemes
- Overly minimalist/cold design
- Too many animations or flashy effects
- Hard-to-read thin fonts
- Cluttered, cramped layouts

## Design Principles

1. **Breathe & Space:** Generous whitespace creates a calm, unhurried shopping experience
2. **Warmth Over Cool:** Every color choice should feel warm and inviting
3. **Typography First:** Text must be effortless to read - this is an information interface
4. **Natural Texture:** Subtle organic shapes and soft edges over sharp corners
5. **Authentic Imagery:** Real farm photos with natural lighting, not stock-looking
6. **Trust Through Transparency:** Clear information, honest presentation, no tricks

## Implementation Notes

- Use rounded corners (rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl) for organic feel
- Shadows should be soft and warm, never harsh
- Transitions should be smooth and gentle (ease-out, 200-300ms)
- Focus on product photography - let the natural beauty shine
- Mobile experience prioritized - many will shop on phones
